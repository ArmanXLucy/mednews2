Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital and Medical Research Institute




"The ENT department at Fortis Hospitals provides a complete spectrum of medical and surgical services for problems related to ear, nose, throat, head and neck. The team of ENT doctors uses the latest technology and treatment modalities to perform ear and cochlear implants, endoscopic nasal surgeries and skull base surgeries. The team treats conditions as below- Hearing and deafness Ringing in ears Deviated Nasal Septum Sinusitis Rhinitis Sinus related issues Vocal cord related problems Various Cancerous conditions Birth defects Infections in ear Foreign Body removal Reconstruction surgeries Tonsil infection."

Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital is a tertiary-care hospital in Four Bungalows, Mumbai, India. The hospital is named after Kokilaben Ambani, wife of industrialist Dhirubhai Ambani, who was the founder of Reliance Industries.
