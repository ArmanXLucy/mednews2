Gleneagles Hospital Mumbai




Gleneagles Hospital in Parel, Mumbai, India, stands as the premier multispecialty medical centre in the region. Renowned as Western India’s top destination for multi-organ transplants, it is a quaternary care facility offering a comprehensive range of medical services. Specialising in Cardiology, Neurology, Gastroenterology, Hepatology, Nephrology, Critical Care, GI & MAS, Urology, Orthopaedics, General Medicine, Interventional Radiology, Gynaecology, and Multi-organ Transplant, Gleneagles Hospital is dedicated to providing exceptional care across a wide spectrum of specialities
