Park Hospital




"Park Group of Hospitals, equipped with world class facilities has witnessed incredible growth to become one of the most advanced and comprehensive healthcare institutions in the region. The name Park Group of Hospital was coined by Dr. Ajit Gupta and the tradition of his rich legacy is being carried forward by his son Dr. Ankit Gupta, Managing Director. Savvy about the current trends in the healthcare sector, Dr. Ankit Gupta believes in maintaining the great reputation of the Park Group of Hospitals with business acumen for viability while sustaining the legacy of providing world-class medical services to people from all economic backgrounds. Passionate about introducing latest technologies for enabling complex surgical intervention for various super specialities, Dr. Gupta’s visionary zeal was recognized with the ‘Future Leader of Healthcare’ Award conferred by the Vice-President of India."
